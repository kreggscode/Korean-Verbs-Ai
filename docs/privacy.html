Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2024

Welcome to Korean Verbs AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used, time spent, and learning progress.
  • Camera Data: When you use the object scanner feature, we process images locally on your device. Images are not stored or transmitted to our servers.
  • AI Chat Data: Conversations with the AI tutor are processed through Pollinations AI API. We do not store your chat history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our app's functionality
  • To improve and personalize your learning experience
  • To analyze app usage and optimize performance
  • To provide AI-powered tutoring through third-party services
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All verb data is stored locally on your device
  • Learning progress is saved locally using encrypted storage
  • Camera images are processed locally and never uploaded
  • AI chat requests are transmitted securely via HTTPS

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Pollinations AI: For AI chat functionality. Please review their privacy policy at their website.
  • Google ML Kit: For on-device text recognition and object detection. Data is processed locally.
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates.

5. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: Required for the object scanner feature to detect and translate objects
  • Internet: Required for AI chat functionality and app updates
  • Vibrate: Used for haptic feedback during interactions

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

All user data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing app data through your device settings.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out of AI features by not using the AI chat functionality
  • Deny camera permissions (scanner feature will be unavailable)

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
